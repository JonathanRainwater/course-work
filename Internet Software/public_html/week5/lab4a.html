Our Story

We no longer speak of the terrible crimes previously committed by the Reformed; it is enough to simply acknowledge that the severity of their misdeeds resulted in life sentences for every last one of them. Fortunately, thanks to an innovative new prison-reform program, these skilled musicians are being allowed to share their gifts with the world once again, all while having the opportunity to demonstrate their good behavior to the parole board by peacefully performing in the public presence of those decent people of the free-world that they once so mercilessly victimized.
